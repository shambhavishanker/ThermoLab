Concept:

The heat absorbed or released is given by q = m · C · ΔT, where:

  • m = mass of substance (g)
  • C = specific heat capacity (J/g·K)
  • ΔT = T₂ − T₁ (°C or K)

If ΔT > 0 → heating (q > 0).
If ΔT < 0 → cooling (q < 0).
